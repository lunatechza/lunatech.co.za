Who We Are

Lunatech is a software development company founded in 2014. By 2026, we have emerged as a leader in AI-driven innovation and the implementation of first-of-its-kind intelligent software solutions.

The team is situated in Jeffreys Bay, South Africa. Although we established ourselves in a sleepy surf town, our global impact is significant. We are involved in major AI and Fintech projects implemented with International partners from around the world.

We work hard but have fun doing so. Our philosophy is built on the belief that an AI-augmented workplace results in inspired, creative, and efficient employees.

Vision 2026

Our core purpose is to architect software that is intelligent, user-centric, and outperforms the competition through AI optimization.

We aim to deliver software that is visually stunning, exceptionally stable, and powered by the latest in machine learning. Every project brings its own set of challenges, and we solve them using the most advanced digital tools available in 2026.
