Our Projects

BLOCKTIX

Blocktix is an Ethereum based, counterfeit-resistant solution for individuals and event hosting businesses to distribute, advertise, and transfer ownership of event tickets or passes.

Blocktix will combine on-chain ticket distribution, a trustless p2p ticket exchange and an incentivised advertising network ting platform that replacesthe fragmented legacy systems currently in use.

The Blocktix platform will have a dramatic effect on the event hosting industry as it will reduce distribution, transfer and promotion costs up to 90% and create real-time accessibility for concertgoers to instantly obtain/sell tickets. These improvements will enhance the concert attendee's purchasing experience and amplify the event host's ability to return a profit.

PARTICL

Particl is an open source, decentralized privacy platform. Built for global person to person and business to person eCommerce. Particl is a privacy platform by design with a native marketplace, currency tokens and suite of encrypted communication tools which offer unfettered access between buyers and sellers worldwide or just down the street. Particl is the first DAPP built on Bitcoin with Confidential Transactions which keep the amounts transferred visible only to participants in the transaction (and those they designate), while still guaranteeing that no more coins can be spent than are available in a cryptographic way.

ChainEX

ChainEX is a Digital Asset Trading Platform for securely trading digital assets against real time markets. ChainEX is a proudly South African based company that provides an environment where South African traders can buy, sell or trade almost 30 different digital assets using South African Rand (ZAR) as the fiat currency. It saves users the hassle of converting to USD first as well as the dealing with numerous banking and federal regulations.
