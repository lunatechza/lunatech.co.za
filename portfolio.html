Our Portfolio

Lunatech provides a full range of custom software development services. Our engineers can help you create the best-in-class business software solutions that perfectly fit your business needs.

ChainEX

ChainEX is a Digital Asset Trading Platform for securely trading digital assets against real time markets. ChainEX is a proudly South African based company that provides an environment where South African traders can buy, sell or trade almost 30 different digital assets using South African Rand (ZAR) as the fiat currency. It saves users the hassle of converting to USD first as well as the dealing with numerous banking and federal regulations.

BLOCKTIX

Blocktix is an Ethereum based, counterfeit-resistant solution for individuals and event hosting businesses to distribute, advertise, and transfer ownership of event tickets or passes.

Blocktix will combine on-chain ticket distribution, a trustless p2p ticket exchange and an incentivised advertising network platform that replaces the fragmented legacy systems currently in use.

PARTICL

Particl is an open source, decentralized privacy platform. Built for global person to person and business to person eCommerce. Particl is a privacy platform by design with a native marketplace, currency tokens and suite of encrypted communication tools which offer unfettered access between buyers and sellers worldwide or just down the street.
