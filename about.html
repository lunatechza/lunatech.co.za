Who we are


Lunatech is a software development company founded in 2014. By 2026, we have emerged as a leader in AI-driven innovation and the implementation of first-of-its-kind intelligent software solutions. Being at the forefront of modern technologies, specifically AI and Blockchain, allows us to push the boundaries of what is possible in the digital age.

The team is situated in Jeffreys Bay, South Africa. Although we established ourselves in a sleepy surf town, our global impact is significant. We are involved in major AI and Fintech projects implemented with International partners from around the world. We combine world-class engineering with the relaxed environment of our beautiful town.

We work hard but have fun doing so. Our philosophy is built on the belief that an AI-augmented workplace results in inspired, creative, and efficient employees. Lunatech is committed to ensuring an exciting environment where human creativity and artificial intelligence collaborate to achieve excellence.
